We will have three main areas for preschool, nursery and toddler-aged children, as well as a separate baby room. We have qualified and experienced staff team members who are committed to ensuring that every child is given the opportunity to develop as an individual to enable and encourage them to reach their full potential through praise, respect and positive attitudes.
The early year's foundation stage is organised into the following seven areas of learning: Prime areas (these are the focus learning for children under three years old) Communication and language Physical Development Personal, social and Emotional Development Specific areas (these are the focus after the child has developed in the prime areas of leaming) Literacy Mathematics Understanding the world Expressive arts and design.
Assessment of children is a statutory requirement of the EYFS, therefore, your key person will see what kind of things your child is interested in and then will base their planning for your child's leaming and development around this. You will be able to see your child's individual learning plan at any time, just ask your child's key person.
Our food will be prepared at the nursery, providing your child with home cooked, nutritious, and balanced meals using fresh ingredients and cooked by our chef daily. Due to allergies, we cannot allow foods to be brought in from home. We are happy to support you and your child with toilet training.
